Tomato Soup

A delicious recipe to keep you warm and satiated.

Ingredients

  1. 4 tomatoes
  2. Half of a medium sized onion
  3. 2 garlic cloves
  4. 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  5. Salt, to taste

1.

Cut the tomatoes into cubes, and mince the onion.

2.

Heat the oil on a pan, and put the onions and garlic clove to fry. Wait until the onions have turned golden or brown.

3.

Add the tomatoes and salt, and cook until the mixture turns mushy.

4.

Transfer to a blender jar with the water and blend it until it is smooth.
