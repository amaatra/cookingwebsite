Sandwich Wrap

A versatile recipe that is simple yet filling

Ingredients

  1. Miscellaneous vegetables
  2. 3-5 leaves of lettuce
  3. A tortilla/roti/chapati or another flatbread
  4. Sauce of your choice

1.

Slice the vegetables you have chosen into vertical strips. Alternatively, you can julienne them.

2.

Smear the sauce of your choice over the flatbread, covering it completely. Place the lettuce in the center of the flatbread/

3.

Put the other vegetables on the lettuce, making sure they are closely packed together.

4.

Carefully fold up the bottom of the flatbread - this is to ensure that the contents do not spill out. Then, slowly roll up the flatbread.
