Boiling Vegetables

Have you ever wondered the best way to boil vegetables? Well, you can learn with these simple steps!

Note: Different vegetables have specific rules on how they should be prepared before they are boiled. Some common vegetables that you might boil are mentioned here, but it is always better to check!

1.

Trim the tough parts of the vegetable away. For vegetables like brocolli and green beans, you must take off the tough, inedible parts before boiling. For corn, you should take off the stem and remove the green leaves. For leafy greens, you should remove the thick stem and the core.

2.

Cut the vegetables into similar sized pieces. This is so that each piece can be cooked equally. If you cut smaller pieces, you can sometimes fit more vegetables into a pot.

3.

Pour water into a large pot, then add a pinch of salt. The salt can add flavour, and slightly increases the boiling point of water.

4.

Place the vegetables into the pot and wait for them to boil. Cabbage may take 5-10 minutes, while green beans can take 5-15 minutes. Corn takes 5 minutes, and brocolli only takes 3-4 minutes.
